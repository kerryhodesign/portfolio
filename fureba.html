Fureba

a culinary guide for foodies traveling to japan.

Overview

A cuisine tourist guide for those who are traveling to Japan. Fureba allows users to explore different cuisines, engage with the foodie community, and plan meals they want to try. The website allows for community interaction where fellow foodies share, reply, and recommend each other locations, cuisines, photos, and videos. Travelers can bookmark specific cuisines and plan their meals along with their adventure. Fureba's responsive website is made in Webflow.
