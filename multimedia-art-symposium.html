Multimedia Art Symposium

creative passions coming together.

Overview

Multimedia Art Symposium 2019, held in Montreal, includes various artistic forms like art installations, music, film, and fine art. The symposium’s branding is an abstraction of Montreal’s clover-shaped logo and the reflection of multimedia arts through objects like a film strip, paint pans, audio levels, and a paintbrush. The clovers reveal heart shapes that express people’s passion and putting their hearts in their art form.
