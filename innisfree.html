Innisfree Catalogue

exuding natural, pure, and fresh skincare.

Overview

Innisfree is a Korean skincare company that focuses on using natural ingredients from Jeju Island. The multipage product catalogue is accompanied by Innisfree's online shop. While following the branding of Innisfree, the catalogue expresses the key importance of Korean skincare, youthfulness, purity, and freshness. The catalogue design uses a minimal layout that follows the colour scheme of their product lines and icons.
