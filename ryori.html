Ryori

the happiness of japanese inspired food service.

Overview

Ryori is a Japanese inspired food service that brings in family-friendly food service and creates quick meals designed by chefs and cooking class offerings. The logo creates an ambiguous representation in the beauty of how individuals can recognize it differently, like the shape of the rising sun, the Japanese character of ‘no’ or either the roundness of a sushi roll, ramen bowl. The green and orange are inspired by the Japanese kimonos representing energy and happiness which is the family-friendly service Ryori provides.
